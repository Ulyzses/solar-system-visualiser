VENUS

This is Earth's twin, Venus -- being the one closest and having nearly the same size as Earth. This rock filled with a very deadly atmosphere is named after the Roman goddess of love and beauty. It is the hottest among the eight planets in the solar system having a maximum temperature of roughly 471 degrees Celsius. To humans, it is also called the Morning and Evening star -- it's so bright it can be seen in the morning.

Diameter: 12,104 km

Mass: 4.87 x 1024 kg

Natural Satellites: None

Orbit Distance: 108,209475 km (0.73 AU)

Revolution: 225 Earth Days

Surface Temperature: 462°C (can reach 471°C)
