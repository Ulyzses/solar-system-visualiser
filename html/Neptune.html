NEPTUNE

This gas giant is named Neptune, after the Roman god of the Sea. It is the planet farthest from the sun. Because it's so far away, ancient civilizations never really saw it. This planet has a giant storm too called the Great Dark Spot. Just like the rest of the gaseous planets, it also has a ring system -- though faint -- consisting of five rings.

Equatorial Diameter: 49,528 km

Mass: 1.02 × 1026 kg (17 Earths)

Natural Satellites: 14

Rings: 5

Orbit Distance: 4,498,396,441 km (30.10 AU)

Revolution: 60,190 days (164.8 years)

Effective Temperature: -214 °C
