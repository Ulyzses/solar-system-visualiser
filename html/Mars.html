MARS

The Red Planet. The one on which we humans (for some reason) insist on migrating towards and finding other life on: Mars. Named after the Roman god of war, Mars is home to the tallest point in the solar system: Olympus Mons. It is also the only other planet besides our beloved Earth that has polar ice caps. The redness of this other rocky floating mass in space is caused by iron oxide on its surface -- a.k.a rust.

Equatoial Diameter: 6,792 km

Mass: 6.42 x 1023 kg (10.7% Earth)

Natural Satellites: Two (Phobos & Deimos)

Orbit Distance: 227,943,824 km (1.52 AU)

Revolution: 687 Earth Days(1.9 Earth Years)

Surface Temperature: -153°C to 20°C
