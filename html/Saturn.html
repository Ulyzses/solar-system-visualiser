SATURN

This ringed guy is named Saturn, after the father of Jupiter in Roman mythology. Much like Jupiter, it also has some storms brewing on its surface. The theory is that its prominent rings were once a natural satellite torn apart by tidal forces. This ring system is made up of 30+ rings divided into 7 sections.

Equatorial Diameter: 120,536 km

Mass: 5.68 × 1026 kg (95 Earths)

Natural Satellites: 62

Rings: 30+ (7 groups)

Orbit Distance: 1,426,666,422 km (9.54 AU)

Revolution: 10,756 days (29.5 years)

Effective Temperature: -178 °C
