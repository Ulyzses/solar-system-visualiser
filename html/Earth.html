EARTH

Our lovely home that we keep destroying: Earth. This rocky mass floating in space is home to billions of little creatures called humans. Being the only planet with tons of water (around 70% of its surface), it is the only one known to be able to sustain life in the solar system. Earth also happens to be the densest planet with a density of 5.52 g/cm3. Its very strong magnetic field is what protects us tiny humans from solar winds, keeping us alive.

Equatorial Diameter: 12,756 km

Mass: 5.97 x 1024 kg

Natural Satellites: One

Orbit Distance: 149,598,262 km (1 AU)

Revolution: 365.24 Earth Days

Surface Temperature: -88°C to 58°C
