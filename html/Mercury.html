MERCURY

This little guy is called Mercury. Named after the Roman messenger of the gods, it's the closest planet to the sun and the smallest one too. This gives it a relatively low mass and gravitational pull. Because of that, its atmosphere is practically non-existent. No atmosphere means no geological activity, which means there's nothing to clear off all the craters made by asteroids. That's how it got a reputation of being the most cratered planet in the solar system.

Diameter: 4,879 km

Mass: 3.30 x 1023 kg (5.5% earth)

Natural Satellites: None

Orbit Distance: 57,909,227 km (0.39 AU)

Revolution: 88 Earth Days

Surface Temperature: -173°C to 427°C
