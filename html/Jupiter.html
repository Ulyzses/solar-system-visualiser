JUPITER

This huge ball of gas orbiting the sun is called Jupiter, after the Roman god of lightning and thunder. This planet is known for being the largest of the eight and for the huge storm that's been raging for over 300 years -- the Great Red Spot. It is home to the largest natural satellite in the solar system, Ganymede. A lot of people don't know that Jupiter actually has a small ring system consisting of 4 rings.
