THE SUN

The center of our solar system: the sun. This ball of gas provides us with the light and warmth necessary for life to thrive on Earth. Classified as a yellow dwarf, this star burns at a scorching 5,500 °C.

Diameter: 1,392,684 km

Mass: 1.99 × 1030 kg (333,060 Earths)

Orbital Period (Around the Milky Way): 225-250 million years

Rotational Period (Equator): 24 Earth Days

Age: 4.6 billion years

Surface Temperaure: 5,500 °C
